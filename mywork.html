Writer / Producer

Adrenaline is an action-packed crime drama series I've been developing since June 2023. Set in present-day New York City, the story follows a brave young NYC native, Lil J (Aurio Vida), who risks it all to take down the cartel when they threaten to destroy everything he's worked for. I wrote and produced the pilot and wrote two seasons. Directed and co-produced by Caio Lourenço De Melo, Adrenaline stars Aurio Vida, Jamie Gene, Joshua Albano, Emiljano Lamushi, and Ady Van Der Plas in key roles. With gripping action and a raw, real-world storyline, Adrenaline brings high stakes to every moment.

Writer / Actor

The Underdog is an independent film I'm currently developing and starring in. The story follows Lele, an MMA fighter whose biggest challenge out the cage is proving to everyone, including himself, that he belongs. As he battles to revive his self-esteem and fight IQ, he sets his sights on The Underdog tournament, a chance to change his life forever.

Editor

LFL 2024 - Present
Since November 2024, I've worked with the LFL, shooting and editing a variety of content. This includes creating gym interview videos for ELEVATES featuring the fighters, producing high-energy bumpers to hype up the crowd right before the fights, and crafting videos for the venue screen. I also had the opportunity to create aftermovies for the amateur fights at LFL 16. This experience has allowed me to blend my passion for film production with live event coverage, while sharpening my skills in fast-paced, high-stakes environments. It's also been a perfect opportunity to combine my love for mixed martial arts with my work in media.

Acting

After completing an 8-week Acting for Film workshop at NYFA in 2019 at 18 years of age, I began my professional acting journey in 2021. I landed two bit parts and a supporting role in family films. While much of my work has been in this genre, I also see myself fitting into romantic films, dramas, and action movies.
